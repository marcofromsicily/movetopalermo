Seven reasons to move to Palermo


An invitation to business and IT people

The city center


Palermo city center is a lovely pedestrian area full of caffè and boutiques.

The city center has been named Unesco World Heritage.

Mondello beach


Palermo's beach is just a 10 km ride from the city center.

Urban bus 806 operates from the city center to the beach.

You can enter a free seaside area or you can look for paid beach services.

Cultural events


Palermo has been named 2018 italian capital of culture.

Teatro Massimo is one of the most important opera house in the world.

Sicilian food


Sicilian food tastes like the sun and the sea.

Too many restaurants should be named as an excellence, Antica Focacceria San Francesco and San Lorenzo Mercato are just two examples.

Fashion


Palermo is still in Italy and via della Libertà hosts many boutiques, form Prada to Gucci.

Rinascente is the most elegant department store in the world.

Internet connection


Of course you need good infrastructure to work, and Palermo is the node of many submarine internet cables.

There are two internet exchange point, the Sicily HUB and the Open HUB Med .

Palermo Airport


Palermo airport is just 30 km from the city center, connecting you to dozens of european destination and to New York.
